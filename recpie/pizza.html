Pizza

pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a round,
flat base of dough baked in an oven. It is usually
topped with tomato sauce,cheese, and a variety of
ingredients such as pepperoni, vegetables, mushrooms,
olives, or meat. The crust can be thin and crispy or thick
and soft. pizza is often cut into slices and eaten hot. It
is one of the most loved foods around the world.


Pizza ingredients

  • flour
  • water
  • yeast

Preparation steps for pizza

  1. flour, water
  2. yeast, salt
  3. olive oil
